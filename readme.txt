Easily integrate the Chartcook.com tracking code into your Wordpress site to see where your visitors are coming from and what they are doing - all with just the click of a button.
 
== Description ==
 
With this plugin you can easily integrate the [Chartcook.com](https://chartcook.com/) tracking code into your Wordpress site. All you need is a free Chartcook account and your personal tracking ID that comes with it.

After you activated the plugin and stored your tracking ID in the settings, the tracking script is automatically included in all your pages.

= What's Chartcook? =

Chartcook is a traffic and user analytics platform inspired by the beauty of simplicity. Get the full picture, quickly and hassle-free with complete analyses available at the click of a button. Analyze and break down your entire website traffic, understand who visits your website and what content they love.

Sign up for **[Chartcook Free](https://chartcook.com/)** in less than 30 seconds and see for yourself how easy data analytics can be!
 
== Installation ==
 
1. Install the Chartcook Analytics plugin either via the WordPress.org plugin repository or by uploading the files to your server
2. Activate the Chartcook Analytics plugin
3. Navigate to Tools > Chartcook Analytics in your WordPress admin menu and insert your personal tracking ID in the appropriate field. This will automatically include the Chartcook tracking code in all the right places on your Wordpress site
